10 REPAIR ROTATOR CUFF

It is vital to close the rotator cuff after insertion of the nail. Number 2 Ethibond or a permanent suture may be utilized to close the rotator cuff. Generally, two figure®8 sutures are used to close the small longitudinal incision of the rotator cuff. After this the deltoid is closed. The wound is then closed in layers with the deltoid closed with Number One Vicryl and the skin is closed in standard fashion.
