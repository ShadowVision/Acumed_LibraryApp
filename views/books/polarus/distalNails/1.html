Distal Nail Technique

1 PREOPERATIVE PLANNING

Fluoroscopy should be used in all cases. Polarus 3 Nail X-ray Template (90‐0038) are available and should be used preoperatively to aid in implant selection. The nail length should be chosen so that the distal holes are distal to the fracture line and do not obstruct the radial nerve. The Polarus 3 Distal Nails (200‐280 mm) are not left or right specific.
