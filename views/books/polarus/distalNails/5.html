5 ASSEMBLE THE TARGETING GUIDE

Attach the Polarus® C Nail Targeting Connector (80‐1629) to the Polarus® C Proximal Targeting Guide (80‐1628), securing with a Polarus® C Locking Knob (80‐1633). Insert the Locking Bolt (80‐1625) through the barrel of the Targeting Connector (80‐1629).

Note: When removing implant from sterile packaging, take care not to disengage PEEK insert from the head of the nail.

Make sure the internal PEEK sleeve is still fully seated in the head of the nail prior to attaching the targeting guide. Assemble implant onto the targeting connector, aligning the reference mark on the implant and targeting connector.

Note: The slot on the implant is offset to prevent misalignment. When assembled properly, the nail will curve toward the targeting guide.

Tighten the bolt into position with the provided Locking Bolt Finger Wrench (MS‐0611).
