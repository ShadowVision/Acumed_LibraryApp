5 CANAL PREPARATION

The entry site is then prepared using the 10 mm bud drill over the guide wire. Drill with the 10 mm Bud Drill to a depth of approximately 50‐60 mm to allow the nail to pass into the canal. An alternate technique for preparing the canal is to use the Polarus 3 Cannulated Broach (80‐1553). Insert the broach to the level of the last cutting tooth. The lateral side of the broach is designated by the direction of the broach handle. After the bud drill is used, the Polarus 3 Guide Wire Passer (80‐1555) is available to assist in passing the guide wire past the fracture site.
