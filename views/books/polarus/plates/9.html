9 ADDITIONAL SHAFT SCREW INSERTION

For the remaining shaft holes, insert a 4.3 mm screw to secure the plate to the shaft. For locking fixation use round holes. For nonlocking fixation, the screw may be inserted through any oblong slot in the plate.

Use the Drop‐In Plate Drill Guide and the 2.8 mm Surgibit® Short Drill; using the Polarus 3 Depth Gauge (80‐1776) to determine the appropriate length of screw to be used. Using the T15 hexalobe driver, insert a screw of the appropriate length.

Note: If dense bone is encountered,

a 4.3 mm screw tap and tap sleeve may be used to further prepare the hole.
